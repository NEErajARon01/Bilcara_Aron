Introducing Trader Wizard 2023

Trader Wizard 2023 is a comprehensive guide to trading in various financial markets. The book is a superior presentation of many aspects of trading and investment strategies to those Bill Cara introduced in his original (2008) hardcover book Lessons From the Trader Wizard. The author also delves into many of the major changes observed in financial markets that followed the 2008 banking system collapse. This 392-page book is divided into seven parts and organized by chapters and sections:

  • Part 1

    What is the Market? What is Trading? This part introduces financial markets and trading, helping readers understand their place in the market and whether they want to pursue trading as a full-time career. It also emphasizes the importance of developing a support system and controlling one’s trading decisions.
